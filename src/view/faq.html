FACTS AND QUESTIONS


Shipping Info

We ship worldwide and guarantee shipment within a week. The products come in cases and are packed with cooling packs to ensure freshness in maintained. The shipping price is a flat rate of $10 for all products.


Returns

If you are unhappy or have any issues with your shipment please return it for a full refund (minus shipping costs). We take returns within two weeks of the purchase date. Please ship returns back at your expense to The Exotic Fruit Company (see the return address included with your shipment).


Freshness Guarantee

We guarantee that you will receive your items fresh and free from rot. We ship within a week an include cooling packs in order to ensure freshness. If you have any issues please return the product for a refund (within two weeks of purchase date at your expense).
